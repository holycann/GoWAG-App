GoWAG Color Palette

Light Theme:
- Primary: #7C9473 (Green)
- Secondary: #CFDAC8 (Light Green)
- Background: #E8EAE6 (Off-White)
- Muted: #CDD0CB (Light Gray)

Dark Theme:
- Primary: #092635 (Dark Blue)
- Secondary: #1B4242 (Dark Teal)
- Accent: #5C8374 (Medium Green)
- Text: #9EC8B9 (Light Teal)

Usage:
- Light theme uses earthy green tones for a natural, calming interface
- Dark theme uses deep blue-green tones for a professional, modern look
- Both themes maintain high contrast for readability
- Accent colors are used for interactive elements and highlights
